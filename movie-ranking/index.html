The Best Movies According to Christian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avengers - Endgame

The culmination of twenty something films with the most epic superhero climax of all time!

The Grand Budapest Hotel

A beautiful film that explores the basic themes of love, war, and compassion in the quirkiest of ways.

Schindler's List

A deep exploration behind the Jewish people who were persecuted, their captors, and their saviors.
